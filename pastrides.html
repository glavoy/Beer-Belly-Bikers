West Nile

This 1200km trip took us through the northern part of Murchison Falls National Park, the largest national park in Uganda. From there, we went on to northwest Uganda where we crossed the River Nile. Riding around this region, we almost touched the borders of both the Democratic Republic of Congo and South Sudan. On the way back down we had to load the bikes on a ferry for a river crossing.
